Aapki 2 aavshyakta hai ek sansar aapko hum sansar se alag nahi kar skte
kyuki aapke sharir ko roti ki jarurat h to nokri ki jarurat padegi
aapke sharir ko ghar ki jarurat hai to pankhe ki or char diwaro ki jarurat padegi
aapko paani ki jarurat h to ghade ki jarurat bhi padegi


ek aapke sharir ki aavshyakta hai or ek aapki aavshyakta h to 
sharir ki aavshyakta hai hawa,roti,pani,aawaas,vastra ye sharir ki aavshyakta hai
sharir ki aavshyakta se 10 guni chij jin logo ke paas h
1000 guni chij jin logo k paas hai 
unko bhi shanti nahi mili kyuki unhone apni aavshyakta ko nahi jana

to ek sharir ki aavshyakta h jo sansar ke saadhno se puri hoti hai
dusri tumhari aavshyakta h jo parmatma se puri hoti hai 
tumhari aavshyakta hai amarta ka aanand
tumhari aavshyakta hai prabhu se mulakat
or prabhu ki aavshyakta hai tumhare se mulakat

mene kai puran,shastra sune lekin mene aisa kahi nahi suna ki
bhagwan mohan thal k bhuke hai
bhagwan laddu pede k bhuke hai
bhagwan chappan bhog ke bhuke hai

lekin humne ye jaroor suna h ki bhagwan bhukhe hai
or bhukh mitane ki takat tumhare m h ye mene suna hai 
aap bhagwan ki bhukh mita sakte h or apna janam maran mita sakte hai 
dono chije aapke paas hai
